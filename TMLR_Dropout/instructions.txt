Here we train CNN and ResNet-18 models with dropout and perform MASC and VeLPIC over the layers of the network during training.


Model_dropout_training.py code has training CNN and ResNet-18 with dropout 
MASC_dropout.py code has MASC for CNN and ResNet-18 with dropout corrupt subspace during training layerwise 
VeLPIC_dropout.py code has VeLPIC for CNN and ResNet-18 with dropout corrupt subspace during training layerwise 

ResNet18 specific codes are avaible in extra folder.
ResNet18_dropout_training.py code has training ResNet-18-CIFAR10 with dropout for 100% training accuracy 
MASC_dropout_ResNet.py code has MASC for ResNet-18-CIFAR10 with dropout corrupt subspace during training layerwise 
VeLPIC_dropout_ResNet.py code has VeLPIC for ResNet-18-CIFAR10 with dropout corrupt subspace during training layerwise
